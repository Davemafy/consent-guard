Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a one-time fee or a subscription?

All of our packages are a **one-time fee**. We believe in providing a permanent solution, not a recurring monthly bill. You pay once and the protection is yours.

Will this slow down my store?

No. In fact, our 'Ultimate Upgrade' package is specifically designed to **increase your store's speed**. By replacing compromised code and optimizing your theme, we improve your Core Web Vitals and overall performance. The 'Essential' toolkit runs asynchronously and is optimized for minimal impact.

Why do I need this? My store seems fine.

Most stores "seem fine" while silently violating laws like GDPR and CCPA by tracking customer data without consent. This leads to fines, ad account suspensions, and inaccurate analytics. Our toolkit fixes these invisible (but very expensive) problems and turns your store into a trustworthy business.

How long does the 'Ultimate Upgrade' take?

Implementation for the Ultimate Upgrade typically takes **1-2 weeks**. This includes consultation, premium theme selection, manual hard-coding, speed optimization, and rigorous testing before we go live. Protection starts immediately upon project commencement.
